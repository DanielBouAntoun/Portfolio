Lilys Garden

My experience working on Lilys Garden was quite special, as it was the first time I actually got my hands on a major project from a game company. The time I spent there had showed me a lot on how a team works together with different responsibilities and skills. The work I did for this game would range from: level creation, creating hints, maintenance for levels that were too easy or hard, organising the database and in my later years occasionally training some of the newer level designers.

Penny & Flo

I spent the majority of my time with the company working on Lilys Garden but on occasion I would switch over to different projects. Penny & Flo was one of the main ones I would switch over to help with, I took more responsibilities with this game because of the experience I had got from Lilys Garden.

Simons Cat: Story Time

Mirror Me

January 2017 - June 2017

Shield my Heart

November 2016 - January 2017
This was a game that went along with my Thesis which was about how we communicate differently in games. This is a two player game were one person takes the role of the heart which is trying to make it from the left side of the screen to the right while avoiding the danders and the shield whose job it is to protect the heart and is invulnerable to everything. It was this game I started to develop interest for level design and pixel art.
